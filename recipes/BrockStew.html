Brock's Lazy Boy No-Chew Stew

A creamy stew featured in the Pokemon anime

This nostalgic and hearty stew debuted in Pokemon: The First Movie. One of Brock's specialties, this dish features chunks of carrots, potatoes, onions, ham, and broccoli. Be sure to fill up on it for your next adventure!

Ingredients

  • 6 red skin potatoes, 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 cup onion, sweet or yellow
  • 1/2 cup of peas
  • 1/2 cup of carrots, chopped or diced
  • 1 1/2 cups of ham, cubed
  • 2 cups of broccoli
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 Tbsp garlic, minced
  • 3 Tbsp flour
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 4 Tbsp cream cheese
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (mozzarella recommended)
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large soup pot over medium heat. Add potatoes and onions and sautee for 5-8 minutes, until potatoes are slightly brown and onions are softened.
  2. Stir in the garlic, peas, carrots, and ham. Sautee for another 2-3 minutes until ham is slightly brown. Stir in brocolli.
  3. Add the flour and stir, coating all ingredients. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir in the cream cheese until melted. Then stir in the shredded cheese until melted.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste and simmer for at least 20 minutes to marry the flavors and further soften the vegetables.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
