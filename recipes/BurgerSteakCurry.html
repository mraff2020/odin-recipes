Burger Steak Curry

A delicious curry featuring tender beef patties

Meat and curry fans rejoice! This dish was first popularized in Pokemon Sword and Shield, and variations can now be seen in Pokemon Sleep. Can be served with rice, mashed potatoes, or even french fries!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, grated
  • 1 garlic clove, grated
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (panko recommended)
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • small amount of olive oil for brushing
  • grill seasoning (optional)
  • 1 green onion, diced
  • A pinch of grated ginger
  • 1 cup chicken or beef broth
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 2 cubes of brown curry mix
  • small amount of browning sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix the ground beef together with the onion, 1/2 of the garlic, breadcrumbs, salt, and pepper. Form into 4 small patty shapes.
  2. Brush the patties with olive oil. Grill seasoning can be added to help form a crust later on.
  3. Combine the rest of the grated garlic with the green onion and ginger in a seperate bowl. This will help season the curry sauce.
  4. In a medium frying pan, brown the patties over medium-high heat, cooking for about 4 minutes on each side. Alternately, you can use a grill pan to get those grill marks like in the game.
  5. In a small sauce pan, melt the butter over medium-high heat. Add the green onion and ginger and brown for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add the broth and curry cubes. Stir occasionally as the cubes melt. Heat to a boil, and continue stirring periodically until the curry thickens.
  7. To plate, place the patties into a pool of curry. Some of it can be spooned on top. Serve with your favorite rice (or whatever you like to eat curry with!)
