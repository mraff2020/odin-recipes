Lava Cookies

Gooey and chewy cookies sure to lift your spirits!

This delightful cookie hails from Lavaridge Town in Hoenn. In the core series Pokemon games, these sweet treats cure status conditions. In real life, they can help cure a bad mood!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almonds, chopped coarsely
  • 1 Tbsp flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 2 1/2 Tbsp heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp apricot jam
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, salt, cinammon, and cayenne pepper together with the almonds.
  2. In a small pot, combine sugar, butter, heavy cream, and apricot jam. Cook over low heat until all ingredients are mixed and melted. Remove from heat once bubbles form.
  3. Add the dry ingredients into the pot. Once the mixture is put together, remove from the pot and put back into the dry ingredients bowl. Let sit for 30 minutes.
  4. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper. Scoop the mixture 1/2 Tbsp at a time onto the sheet. Flatten the cookies slightly with a wet hand or spatula. Note: Do not crowd the pan, as these will widen as they bake!
  5. Bake at 375(F) for 8-10 minutes.
  6. Allow to cool for at least 10 minutes.
  7. Melt the chocolate chips in the microwave using a heatproof bowl in increments of 25 seconds.
  8. Spread the melted chocolate in a square shape from the center of each cookie to the edge to simulate the shape of nori.
  9. Allow the chocolate to re-harden then serve.
